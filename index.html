About

As a highly motivated individual, I've always been deeply intrigued by the ever-evolving world of technology. Though my professional journey began in construction, where I dedicated five years, my fascination with tech's transformative power only grew. I've come to believe that technology is all-encompassing and truly represents the future for everyone. This realization, perhaps unexpected from someone with a construction background, has led me to redirect my focus, recognizing that my time and skills are most valuably invested in the tech industry, a field I find more compelling and impactful than any other.

Born and raised in Logan, Utah, I have a strong connection to this state, even after experiencing life in places like California, Maryland, and Indiana. There's something about Utah—from its snowy winters to its summer heat—that always calls me back. Beyond my love for the diverse landscapes, I am incredibly passionate about technology and clearly see the immense value and opportunity it brings. I believe tech is a powerful force for positive change, not just for large corporations or small startups, but for every individual and every community in between.
